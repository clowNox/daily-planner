# 📋 Daily Planner — AI-Powered Task Priority Manager

A mobile-first daily planning web app that helps you organize your tasks, track your progress, and build better productivity habits — day by day.

Live app: **https://clownox.github.io/daily-planner**

---

## ✨ Features

### Planner
- **Smart Priority Scoring** — Each task is automatically scored 0–100 based on urgency (due date) and category. Tasks are ranked High, Medium, or Low priority instantly when added.
- **8 Task Categories** — Work, Essential, Health, Family, Grooming, Pet, Friends, Business — each color-coded for quick scanning.
- **Daily Planning** — Plan tomorrow's to-do list every evening. Add tasks throughout the day anytime.
- **Task Completion** — Check off tasks as you complete them. Completed tasks move to a separate "Done" section.
- **Progress Bar** — A live progress bar shows your daily completion percentage at a glance.
- **Filter by Category** — Focus on one area at a time using the category filter bar.
- **End Day** — When your day is done, tap "End Day" to review a summary of what you completed, then save and clear for tomorrow.

### Stats
- **Overview Dashboard** — See total days tracked, current streak, total tasks completed, and average daily completion rate.
- **Best Day** — Your highest-performing day is highlighted with task count and completion rate.
- **Category Breakdown** — Visual breakdown of which categories you complete most tasks in, with progress bars per category.

### History
- **Daily Log** — Every day you end is saved with a full record of tasks, completion status, and your score.
- **Up to 30 days** of history stored locally on your device.

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Add a task** — Type a task name, pick a due date, and choose a category. It gets scored and sorted instantly.
2. **Work through your list** — Tasks are ranked from most to least urgent. Check them off as you go.
3. **End your day** — Tap "End Day" to save a summary to History and clear the list for tomorrow.
4. **Track your progress** — Visit the Stats tab to see your streaks, completion rates, and category habits over time.

## 📱 Install as a Mobile App

1. Open the app link in **Safari** (iPhone) or **Chrome** (Android)
2. Tap the **Share** button
3. Select **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. The app will appear on your home screen like a native app — no App Store needed

## 💾 Data & Privacy

- All data is stored **locally on your device** using localStorage
- Nothing is sent to any server
- Works fully offline after the first load
- No account, no login, no tracking
